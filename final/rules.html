Initially, the cave (the board) is empty

Player ■ and player □ move in an alternate fashion, starting with ■.
So ■ starts, followed by □, then ■ again,
then □ again, ...

- Because there are two big magnets on each side of the cave, a player can
only place a brick on an empty cell
of the cave provided that the brick is stacked directly on the left or right wall,
or is stacked to the left or the
right of another brick (of any color).

As soon as one player is able to align 5 consecutive bricks in a row, in a column or
in a diagonal, then this player wins the game.

- If no player is able to achieve a winning configuration and the board is full,
then the game stops and there is a tie.
